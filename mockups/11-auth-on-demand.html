approach b

auth on demand — silent gate

The sidebar looks identical to the logged-in experience — filter, "add a find" button, all present. Guests can see exactly what contributing looks like. When they click "add a find", the sidebar shows a compact inline sign-in form instead of the pin submission form. After signing in, it drops straight into the add-pin flow. The map and filter are always accessible.

in favour guests see the full feature set before signing up
auth feels contextual — triggered by intent
no "why is this button greyed out?" confusion
seamless flow: sign in → immediately add pin
trade-offs clicking "add a find" unexpectedly shows login (mild surprise)
slightly more complex state management
"my pins" button is meaningless for guests (needs to be hidden)
