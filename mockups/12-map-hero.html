approach c

map hero — banner + contextual sign-in

The map is the first and only thing you see. A slim (40px) sticky banner at the top carries the branding and sign-in / create account buttons. The sidebar is collapsed by default; its hamburger tab is clickable to open the filter panel. "Add a find" is in that panel but triggers auth if guest. Zero sign-in pressure until the user decides they want to contribute.

in favour most impressive first impression — the map speaks for itself
zero sign-in friction — banner is easily ignored
filter still accessible (sidebar tab or FAB on mobile)
works well as a "try before you commit" product
trade-offs filter harder to discover (sidebar collapsed by default)
banner adds vertical real estate (especially on mobile)
